📌 Bulletin Board Chrome Extension
A simple bulletin board that lives right in your browser as a Chrome extension.

📥 Step 1 — Download the Files

Click the green "Code" button at the top of this page
Click "Download ZIP"
Once downloaded, unzip the folder somewhere on your computer (like your Desktop)


🔧 Step 2 — Load it into Chrome

Open Google Chrome
In the address bar, type chrome://extensions and press Enter
In the top right corner, toggle on "Developer Mode"
Click "Load unpacked"
Select the unzipped folder from Step 1
Done! Open a new tab to view the Bulletin Board
